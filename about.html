About us

The objectives of sport management, from an organizational point of view, are many and varied. They can be financial, but also marketing or communication. It can also be a question of satisfying technical, sporting or sanitary requirements. Finally, they can be economic, financial or patrimonial.

Sport management is a discipline that brings together different areas of expertise and responsibility. In general, sport management is considered to encompass the organizational, financial, marketing and legal aspects of sport. However, depending on the nature and size of the sport organization, managers may have more or less extensive responsibilities. The purpose of sport management is to ensure the smooth running of the organization by setting up an appropriate structure and defining the roles and responsibilities of each member of the organization. It is also about managing the different services (administrative, logistic, technical) within the organization.
